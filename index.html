About Skillencio

Degrees alone don't land jobs anymore. Employers want skills—real-world experience, industry exposure, and practical expertise. But traditional education falls short, leaving graduates unprepared. Skillencio changes that.

At Skillencio, we bridge this gap by integrating cutting-edge technology, AI-driven learning analytics, and industry-aligned training programs that equip students with the right skills to thrive in the workforce.

We partner with universities, colleges, and students to deliver structured, technology powered employability programs that go beyond theory—focusing on practical, career ready skills with proven placement outcomes.

Bridging The Employability Gap

More graduates, fewer jobs. The problem? Classroom learning isn't enough— students need real-world skills to get hired. Without hands-on experience and career-focused training, graduates struggle to get hired. Skillencio bridges this gap.

  • 50.3% of Indian graduates are not employable (India Skills Report 2023).
  • Only 45% are suitable for technical roles (Mercer | Mettl Report).
  • NASSCOM highlights a critical skills gap in engineering and IT.

At Skillencio, we do not just teach skills—we ensure students acquire, apply, and get placed through a proven technology-driven approach.
