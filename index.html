Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this really free?

Yes. Skillencio is offering these premium mock tests at no cost to selected engineering colleges as part of our employability initiative.

How many students can take the test from our institution?

There is no limit. Once approved, your institution will receive a unique link that can be shared with all students.

What is the test format?

The mock tests follow the latest TCS NQT 2025 pattern, covering:

  • Foundation Section (75 minutes) – Numerical Ability, Verbal Ability, Logical Reasoning

  • Advanced Section (115 minutes, mandatory for TCS Digital roles) – Advanced Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, and Coding

How do students access the tests?

Once your institution is approved, you will receive a unique test registration link. Students can access the tests online using their laptops, desktops, or mobile devices.

How long does it take to get access after registering?

Our team will review your request within 48 hours. If approved, you will receive access details via email.

Can faculty track student performance?

Yes. Institutions can request performance analytics to monitor student progress and guide preparation strategies.

How long will this free offer be available?

This is a limited-time offer, available to only 25 selected engineering colleges.

How do we get started?

Simply submit your institution’s details, and we will review your application. If selected, you will receive access within 48 hours.
