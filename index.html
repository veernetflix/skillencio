TCS NQT 2025 Exam Pattern Covered in Our Mocks

Skillencio's mock tests are structured to provide authentic practice, covering all sections of the TCS NQT 2025 exam.

These mock tests match the latest TCS NQT pattern, ensuring students get realistic exam experience and improve their performance.

Foundation Section (75 minutes)

  • Numerical Ability : 20 questions (25 minutes) – Arithmetic, percentages, profit-loss, data interpretation

  • Verbal Ability : 25 questions (25 minutes) – Grammar, reading comprehension, sentence completion

  • Reasoning Ability : 20 questions (25 minutes) – Logical puzzles, analogies, spatial reasoning

Advanced Section (115 minutes, mandatory for TCS Digital roles)

  • Advanced Quantitative Aptitude & Logical Reasoning: 15 questions (25 minutes) – Complex arrangements, probability, data sufficiency

  • Coding: 3 coding problems (90 minutes) – Algorithm-based coding problems in C, C++, Java, Python
